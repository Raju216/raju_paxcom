Data Insights

Deep dive into your data to drive growth strategies

Data alone will not be of any value until it says something. Our services will empower you with quick access to key performance indicators of your ecommerce business. From covering basic insights on due orders, SLA breached orders to advanced decision driving analytics on lost sales because of out of stock products, top reasons for poor ratings and basket of information which help to improve your ecommerce business.

What we solve for you?
  • Multiple Dashboards :- We provide multiple dashboards on critical data.
  • Performance indicators :- Insights on the performance indicators by which your sales will be affected like on fulfilment of orders, sales on multiple channel  and products which are out of stock .
